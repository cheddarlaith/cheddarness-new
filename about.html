Who is Cheddarness?

Born and raised in Beirut, Lebanon, Cheddar has inherently been surrounded by a vibrant and ambitious environment. He has involved himself in numerous creative and arduous activities since his youth, with a tenacity for understanding the culture of all types of arts and knowledge. At the age of 21, he has devoted his time to directing music videos, encapsulating evocative moments through film photography, and creating various types of electronic music.

Cheddar has always seen the beauty in color and movement, looking to spread his ideas and innovations to the world through all types of art with an unwavering attitude.

Pursuing Psychology, Philosophy, and Media-Arts–Design during his time as an undergraduate, Cheddar attempts at blending the mind with music through his works and performances, advocating for experiencing Techno and electronic music through an introspective and mindful lens.
